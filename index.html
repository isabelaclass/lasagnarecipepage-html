Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Baked to perfection, this hearty and flavorful dish is a favorite comfort food around the world. Whether prepared with traditional ingredients or creative variations, lasagna is a versatile meal that brings people together at the table.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef (or a mix of beef)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Ingredients for the Béchamel Sauce:

  1. 2 tablespoons butter
  2. 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  3. 2 cups milk
  4. A pinch of nutmeg (optional)
  5. Salt and pepper to taste

Other Ingredients:

  1. 12 lasagna sheets (pre-cooked or fresh)
  2. 200g ricotta cheese (optional)
  3. 200g mozzarella cheese, shredded
  4. 0g Parmesan cheese, grated

Instructions

    Prepare the Meat Sauce

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and cook until soft.
  3. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for another 30 seconds.
  4. Add the ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat if needed.
  5. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  6. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

    Prepare the Béchamel Sauce

  1. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the flour and stir continuously for about 1 minute.
  3. Slowly pour in the milk while whisking to avoid lumps.
  4. Cook until the sauce thickens. Add salt, pepper, and nutmeg if using.
  5. Remove from heat and set aside.

    Assemble the Lasagna

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom of a baking dish.
  3. Add a layer of lasagna sheets.
  4. Spread some meat sauce over the pasta, followed by a layer of béchamel.
  5. Sprinkle some mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with cheese on top.

    Bake the Lasagna

  1. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  2. Remove the foil and bake for another 15 minutes or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  3. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
